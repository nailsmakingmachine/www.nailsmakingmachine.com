Frequently Asked Questions

Expert answers to common queries regarding our wire nail manufacturing plants and global export capabilities.

What is the production capacity of your high-speed nail making machines?

Our automated high-speed wire nail making machines are engineered for massive industrial scale, capable of producing between 400 to 1,200 precision-cut nails per minute, depending on the specific wire gauge (8 SWG to 14 SWG) and nail length parameters.

Do you export complete nail manufacturing plants globally?

Yes, NAILS MAKING MACHINE is a premier exporter based in Rajkot, India. We ship, install, and commission complete turnkey nail manufacturing plants—including wire drawing machines and polishing drums—to over 35 countries across Africa, the Middle East, and Asia.

Are spare parts and cutting tools readily available?

Absolutely. We maintain a robust global supply chain for all spare parts, including tungsten carbide dies, cutting knives, and punch pins. We ensure our international clients experience zero prolonged downtime by expediting heavy-duty spare components directly from our manufacturing hub.

What kind of warranty and technical support do you provide?

We provide a comprehensive manufacturing warranty against any mechanical defects. Furthermore, our engineering team offers 24/7 lifetime technical support, including video-guided troubleshooting and detailed maintenance manuals to keep your machinery running at factory efficiency.
